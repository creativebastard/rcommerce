## Permitted Uses

You may use the Trademarks without prior written permission only in the
following circumstances:

### 1. Referring to the Software

You may use "R Commerce" to refer to the software product in:
- Blog posts and articles
- Documentation and tutorials
- Conference presentations
- Social media posts
- Compatibility statements

**Requirements:**
- Use the exact form: "R Commerce" (not "RCommerce", "rcommerce", etc.)
- Include the ® symbol on first use in prominent locations
- Include attribution: "R Commerce is a trademark of PDG Global Limited"

### 2. Compatibility Statements

You may state that your product is "Compatible with R Commerce" or "Works with
R Commerce" provided that:
- Your product actually works with R Commerce
- You don't imply endorsement by us
- You don't use our logo without permission

### 3. Forks and Derivatives (AGPL License)

If you fork R Commerce under AGPL-3.0:
- You MUST change the name (cannot use "R Commerce")
- You MUST create your own logo and branding
- You MAY state "Based on R Commerce" in small text
- You MAY NOT use our trademarks in your project name

**Suggested naming:**
- ✅ "CommercePro - based on R Commerce"
- ✅ "OpenCart Plus (R Commerce fork)"
- ❌ "R Commerce Pro"
- ❌ "R Commerce Enterprise"

## Logo Usage Guidelines

### Official Logo

The R Commerce logo consists of:
- The stylized "R" mark
- The wordmark "R Commerce"
- The combination of both

### Correct Usage

✅ **Do:**
- Use the official logo files from our brand kit
- Maintain clear space around the logo
- Use on contrasting backgrounds
- Scale proportionally

❌ **Don't:**
- Change the colors
- Add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Rotate or tilt
- Stretch or distort
- Place on busy backgrounds

- **Primary:** #4F46E5 (Indigo)
- **Secondary:** #10B981 (Emerald)

- **Light:** #FFFFFF (White)

## Reporting Trademark Abuse

If you see misuse of R Commerce trademarks, please report it:

**Email:** trademark@rcommerce.dev
**Subject:** Trademark Violation Report

Include:
- Description of the violation
- URL or location of the violation
- Your contact information
- Any supporting documentation

## Requesting Trademark Permission

To request permission for a use not covered here:

**Email:** trademark@rcommerce.dev
**Subject:** Trademark Usage Request

Include:
- Your name and company
- Description of intended use
- Samples or mockups
- Duration of use
- Geographic scope

We typically respond within 5-7 business days.

result in:

3. Social media account reporting
4. Legal action for trademark infringement
